## Overview

Jacob's Distributor is a full-stack music distribution platform built for independent artists who want to get their music everywhere without giving up their rights. Upload your tracks once and distribute to Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, and 150+ platforms worldwide — keeping 100% of your rights.

## What I Built

- **Landing page** with sign-in flow powered by Supabase authentication
- **Artist dashboard** showing total streams, monthly growth, active listeners, and total earnings — all with percentage growth indicators
- **Streams Over Time** and **Earnings Breakdown** chart sections for visualizing platform performance
- **Profile page** with completeness tracker, public artist preview, quick actions, and social link management
- **Settings page** covering account info, security (2FA), notification preferences, and payout method configuration via Stripe
- **4-step release upload flow** — Upload → Details → Platforms → Review — supporting MP3, WAV, and FLAC

## Tech Stack

- **Next.js 14 + TypeScript** — framework
- **Tailwind CSS** — styling
- **Supabase** — authentication and backend
- **PostgreSQL** — database
- **Vercel** — deployment
